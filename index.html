ayanalogo

Ayana is a two day event which includes technical workshops and a 24 hour Hackathon. Its an open event aimed at students, developers and designers from all across India with an emphasis on Open Source technologies. The technical workshops would consist of hands-on tutorials on popular Open Source technologies that are in vogue, by prominent persona from the Industry as well as noted contributors to open source.

The second day of the event will be dedicated to a 24 hour continuous hackathon, where participants have to design and develop a ‘hack’ within the stipulated 24 hours. The participant can choose to work on any domain of his interest. The best one at the end of the time period will be adjudged the winne

We hereby issue a call to action for developers, designers and innovators to come to PESIT, Bangalore and participate in this most awesome event! Ayana will provide you with everything you need to have fun, be creative and productive, and impress your peers. Show us what you can build with the wealth of technologies and data available on the Internet.
